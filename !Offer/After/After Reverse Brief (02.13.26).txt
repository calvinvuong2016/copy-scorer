MARKET SPECIFICS
What Does This Market Hinge Their Success On?
Avoiding catastrophic financial/emotional burden on their children while maintaining dignity and control over their final arrangements.
The market believes that "success" = their kids NOT crying in a funeral home office while being pressured to spend money they don't have. Success = the family remembering THEM, not the logistics nightmare.

What Does the Market Have to Give Up By Solving Their Problem?
The comforting delusion that death is "still far away" and they have "plenty of time."
Pre-planning means confronting mortality. It means admitting they're old enough that death is now a practical concern, not an abstract concept. It means giving up the fantasy that they'll "get to it eventually."
They also give up the secret, twisted comfort of imagining their funeral as a scene where everyone realizes how much they mattered. Pre-planning is pragmatic and humble—it admits death is just logistics, not a dramatic movie moment.

1. The Funeral Industry: Predatory pricing, manipulation of grieving families, lack of transparency, turning death into a profit machine
