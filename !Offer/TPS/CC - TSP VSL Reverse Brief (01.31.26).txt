UNIQUE MECHANISM OF THE PROBLEM:
You have TWO types of wrinkles that each need to be addressed individually, and most products only address one.
Dynamic Wrinkles: Caused by repeated facial muscle contractions (smiling, squinting, frowning) that create "expression lines" around the eyes, mouth, and forehead. Over time, these get "etched" into the skin.
Static Wrinkles: Caused by the breakdown of collagen and elastin proteins, which weakens the skin and allows gravity to create sagging, drooping, and volume loss. Free radicals from sun exposure, stress, processed foods, alcohol, smoking, and sugar accelerate this breakdown.
The Hidden Process: "What really causes skin to age isn't gravity… It's something deeper. Something biological. A critical breakdown that happens inside your skin over time. Gravity only makes it worse after that process has already begun."
