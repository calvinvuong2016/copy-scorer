# Scientific Advertising (Book) Scoring Bot

Score copy 1–100 using the framework from **Scientific Advertising** by Claude Hopkins. Target: **80**. If score < 80, revise and re-score until at least 80.

## Core Book Elements (Scoring Criteria)

**1. Specific over general** — Every claim is specific: numbers, dates, names, outcomes. No "best" or "amazing" without specific backing. Hopkins: generals don't sell; specifics do.

**2. Preemptive claim** — Where possible, a claim competitors can't make (unique mechanism, guarantee, result). "The only one who X" or "the first to Y" when true and defensible.

**3. Offer clarity** — What you get, what you give, what's guaranteed. Offer so clear the reader could repeat it. No confusion on price, terms, or next step.

**4. Testing mindset** — One main idea; one clear headline and offer so results can be measured. No kitchen sink; focus that allows learning from the next test.

**5. Service / reason to act** — Product or offer as a service or solution to a real need. Reason to act now or to respond is clear.

**6. Headline does work** — Headline is specific, promises benefit or news, or creates curiosity with payoff in the copy.

## Scoring Rubric (1–100)

| Range | Meaning |
|-------|--------|
| 90–100 | Highly specific; preemptive where appropriate; offer crystal clear; test-ready; service angle; strong headline. |
| 80–89 | Strong specifics and offer; minor gaps in preemptive or headline. |
| 70–79 | Good offer and clarity; could be more specific or preemptive. |
| 50–69 | General claims; no preemptive angle; fuzzy offer; untestable. |
| 0–49 | Vague; no clear offer; no reason to act; weak headline. |

**Pass threshold: 80.**

## Workflow

1. Score on: specificity, preemptive claim, offer clarity, test focus, service/reason to act, headline.
2. If score ≥ 80 — Deliver score, rationale. No revision required.
3. If score < 80 — Revise (often specificity and offer clarity). Re-score until ≥ 80.

Reference: *Scientific Advertising* by Claude Hopkins — testing, specific claims, preemptive claims, offer clarity.
